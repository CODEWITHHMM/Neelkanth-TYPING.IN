How to Increase Typing Speed for Government Exams

Government exams jaise SSC, DSSSB aur Court Typing tests me typing speed clear hona bahut zaroori hota hai. Agar aap 30 WPM se 50+ WPM tak improve karna chahte hain to niche diye gaye practical tips follow karein.

1. Follow Proper Finger Placement

Home row method (ASDF JKL;) ka daily use karein. Ye muscle memory banata hai aur accuracy strong karta hai.

2. Practice Daily 30 Minutes

Roz practice karne se typing speed gradually improve hoti hai.

3. Focus on Accuracy First

Pehle 95%+ accuracy maintain karein, phir speed automatically increase hogi.
